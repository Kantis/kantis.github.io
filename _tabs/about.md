I'm a 38 years old geek, living in Stockholm, Sweden. 🇸🇪 

I have worked as a software engineer the past 15 years, using a mix of technologies. Initially C# and Java, mixed with web technologies (JS, CSS, HTML, etc). Lately more niched on Kotlin backend development. 

I run two businesses. One as a software consultant, and the other selling SaaS-integrations for accounting software.

As a freelancer I'm currently on an assignment for ATG (Aktiebolaget Trav och Galopp) where I've worked on systems for handling horse betting integrations, Casino and Sports betting for the past five years. Using Kotlin, Spring, and Gradle as daily drivers. 

Interested in functional programming, and would love to work more with Ktor and Arrow. 

## My open source work

I have also released a few projects of my own, such as:

- [Mikrom](https://github.com/Kantis/mikrom) - A micro ORM inspired by Dapper, written in Kotlin.
